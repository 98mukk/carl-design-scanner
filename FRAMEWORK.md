# The CARL Framework — Copy · Analyze · Rulebook · Launch
> The Sharingan Scan, sealed into a repeatable jutsu. This is the teachable asset — the app just automates step 1–2.

## The One-Liner (say this when selling it)
**"Great design isn't invented, it's decoded. CARL decodes any elite website into a one-page rulebook you can build from."**

## The 4 Hand Seals
| Seal | Move | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| **C — Copy** | Sharingan | Crawl the target site (Firecrawl grabs HTML + content) |
| **A — Analyze** | Byakugan | Extract the raw signals: colors, fonts, radii, spacing, section order |
| **R — Rulebook** | Hand Seals | Fill the 6-line worksheet — rules, not pixels |
| **L — Launch** | Rasengan | Build YOUR thing from the rulebook (tokens first, then layout) |

## The 6-Line Rulebook (the worksheet — this is the product)
```
1. COLORS   — bg / text / ONE accent
2. FONTS    — heading / body (max 2 families)
3. SHAPE    — corner radius scale / spacing rhythm
4. SECTIONS — top→bottom list
5. THE ONE RULE — what kills the slop on this site
6. STEAL THIS   — the single move to copy
```
Rules 1–4 are mechanical (CARL automates them). Rules 5–6 are taste — that's what you teach.

## Why it's repeatable
Same input type (a URL) → same sequence (C-A-R-L) → same output shape (6-line rulebook). Like a jutsu: fixed hand seals, same result every time. Anyone can run it after one demo.

# Course Packaging (repackage later)
**Course name idea:** *"Steal Like a Sensei: Decode Elite Web Design in 20 Minutes"*

- **Module 1 — The Eye (Copy):** why you scan masters instead of guessing. Live scan of Apple + one hero site with CARL.
- **Module 2 — The Autopsy (Analyze):** reading the CARL report — what colors/fonts/radii actually tell you.
- **Module 3 — The Rulebook:** writing rules 5–6 by hand. Taste reps. 3 graded scans.
- **Module 4 — The Build (Launch):** turn one rulebook into design tokens (`:root` CSS vars) and ship a page.
- **Capstone:** student scans a site in their niche, builds a landing page from the rulebook.

Each module = 1 scan + 1 artifact. The artifacts stack into a personal design vault — that's the retention hook.

# Marketing & Sales Playbook
**Positioning:** Not "an AI website scraper." It's **a taste-training tool**. Scrapers give you data; CARL gives you judgment.

**Who buys:**
1. Freelancers/agencies who need client sites to *not* look templated (your LSD clients — dogfood it).
2. Self-taught devs who can code but can't design (your own origin story = the marketing story).
3. Course students (Module access + CARL bundled).

**Funnel:**
- **Top:** Post before/after scans on X/LinkedIn — "I scanned Apple's homepage. Here's the 6 lines that make it premium." (each scan = free content, infinitely repeatable)
- **Mid:** Free tier — 3 scans/month. Lead magnet: "The 10 Rulebooks" PDF (10 pre-scanned elite sites).
- **Bottom:** $19/mo unlimited scans, or bundled into the course ($149–$249).

**Sales line for LSD client calls:** "Before we design anything, we run CARL on the top 3 sites in your industry. You're not paying for my taste — you're paying for decoded proof of what works."

**Moat:** the tool is a wrapper (anyone can copy it); the **rulebook library + course + your scan commentary** is the moat. Publish scans relentlessly.
